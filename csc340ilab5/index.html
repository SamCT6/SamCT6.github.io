North Plate Offer

North Plate Offer is a small family owned resturant focusing on Alaskan Cuisine. Find us located on the north shore of Kenai River. Head North on Bridge Access Rd. Then take the first left before Kenai Spur Hwy onto Frontage Rd.Next turn left onto Spur View Rd and head straight until you see our parking lot.
